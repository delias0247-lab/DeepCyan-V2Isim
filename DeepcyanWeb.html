The V2ISim Architecture.

Our platform bridges the gap between mobility simulation and artificial intelligence, creating a seamless, containerized virtual testing environment.

1. Mobility & Network Simulation

We utilize SUMO (Simulation of Urban Mobility) and Netedit to create realistic, multi-junction urban environments, synchronizing vehicle movements and simulated V2X network communications in real-time.

2. The TraCI Orchestrator

A custom Python backend uses the TraCI protocol as the central nervous system, extracting live telemetry (speed, queues, latency) from the simulation and feeding it instantly to our predictive models.

3. AI Data Interpreter

Powered by Q-Learning and Deep Q-Learning, our AI continuously evaluates the state vector to output collision probabilities and suggest dynamic traffic signal interventions.

Interactive Web Dashboard.
Actionable Insights.

V2ISim provides researchers and operators with a powerful ReactJS interface to configure scenarios, monitor live traffic, and analyze post-run performance metrics.

Live Visualizations

Watch simulations unfold in real-time. Our canvas displays live vehicle movements, active traffic flow corridors, and immediate UI warnings for predicted collisions or severe congestion.

Dynamic Scenario Control

Users can easily adjust parameters mid-simulation. Speed up or slow down execution, dynamically add or remove vehicles, and toggle between different Reinforcement Learning models on the fly.

Post-Run Analytics

Every simulation run logs high-fidelity time-series data. Export metrics like total queue length, cumulative AI reward, and junction throughput to CSV/JSON, or view them directly on our interactive charts.
